2. **Presale_Customer** - Applied after successful Stripe payment
3. **High_Engagement** - Applied to active email openers/clickers
4. **Survey_Completed** - Applied when someone completes the productivity survey
5. **VIP** - Applied to customers who refer others or provide testimonials

### Automation Rules:
1. **Lead Magnet Signup** → Tag "Lead_Magnet" → Start "Lead Magnet Delivery" sequence
2. **Stripe Payment Successful** → Tag "Presale_Customer" → Start "Presale Onboarding" sequence
3. **Email opened 3+ times** → Tag "High_Engagement"
4. **Survey form submission** → Tag "Survey_Completed" + "Lead_Magnet"
5. **Customer referral** → Tag "VIP" → Send bonus content

### Sequences to Create:
1. **Lead Magnet Delivery** (4 emails over 6 days)
2. **Presale Customer Onboarding** (7 emails over 14 days)
3. **High Engagement Nurture** (Advanced tips and case studies)
4. **Win-Back Campaign** (For inactive subscribers)
5. **VIP Customer Rewards** (Exclusive content and early access)

This comprehensive ConvertKit setup ensures every subscriber receives targeted, valuable content based on their engagement level and purchase status.
